The day felt longer than usual, maybe because the clouds never moved from above.
I kept hearing the soft hum of the ceiling fan, steady and almost hypnotic, while the pages of my notebook filled slowly with uneven lines.
Some words leaned too far to the right, others pressed hard into the paper, leaving faint impressions on the next sheet.
It wasn’t neat, but it felt honest like the way thoughts spill out when you don’t try to polish them.
